## 4. Risk Assessment

- **log-export.sh stash fix**: Low risk. `git stash --quiet` is a no-op when there's nothing to stash. The `|| true` on stash pop prevents failures if stash was empty.
- **log-watcher pattern changes**: Medium risk. Tightened patterns might miss a genuine communication attempt that mentions "marvin" without the `.cz` domain. - **Removed `'ECHO'` pattern**: Low risk. `'echo.*signal'` still catches ECHO protocol signals. Raw `'ECHO'` was matching TCP SYN flag descriptions in firewall logs.
